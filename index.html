Game Rules

1. Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Randomly killing or attacking another player without valid roleplay justification is strictly prohibited.

  • Violence must always have a clear in-character reason.
  • Initiation must be obvious and understandable to all parties.
  • Random or silent attacks are not permitted.

2. Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Using a vehicle as a weapon without proper roleplay justification is forbidden.

  • Vehicles may not be used to intentionally hit or kill players.
  • Driving must reflect real-life limitations and logic.

3. Leave to Avoid Punishment (LTAP)

Disconnecting or leaving the server to avoid roleplay consequences is not allowed.

  • Logging out during active RP counts as LTAP.
  • Crashing must be reported immediately upon return.

4. Fail Roleplay (FRP)

All actions must remain realistic and believable.

  • You must value your life at all times.
  • Unrealistic reactions or behavior break immersion.

5. New Life Rule (NLR)

After dying, your character forgets all events leading up to death.

  • You may not return to the same scene for 5 minutes.
  • Revenge or continuation of previous RP is prohibited.

6. Metagaming

Using out-of-character information in character is not allowed.

  • This includes Discord, streams, or private messages.
  • All information must be obtained in-character.

7. Powergaming

Forcing unrealistic actions or outcomes on others is prohibited.

  • Always allow others to react and roleplay fairly.
  • Actions must be possible within real-world logic.

8. Fear Roleplay

Your character must show realistic fear when threatened.

  • Ignoring weapons or danger is considered Fail RP.

9. Unrealistic Driving

Driving must follow realistic behavior.

  • Excessive off-road driving is prohibited.
  • Stunt driving or ramming is not allowed.

10. Cop Baiting

Intentionally provoking law enforcement to force RP is not allowed.

  • Minor crimes done repeatedly to gain attention are prohibited.

11. Safe Zones

Designated areas where all hostile RP is forbidden.

  • Includes spawn areas, hospitals, and safe zones.

12. Exploiting / Glitch Abuse

Using bugs or exploits for advantage is strictly forbidden.

  • All exploits must be reported to staff immediately.
